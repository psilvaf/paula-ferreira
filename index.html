About me

I'm a post-doc at Observatório Nacional in Rio de Janeiro, my research field is Cosmology.

I work with Large-Scale structure of the universe, especially Baryon ACoustic Oscillations. In astrophysical context, I am involded with photmetric redshift estimation and galaxy morphology (which will be crucial to a future bigger project).

I'm part of the J-PAS collaboration and the Querubines science communication project. Here, there are some links to old projects and written work, as well as, a photometric redshift catalog we tested: Pz Cats.
